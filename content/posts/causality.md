Probability density function:
$$f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}$$

Expected value:
$$E[X] = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x \cdot f(x) \, dx$$

Conditional probability:
$$P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}$$

Bayes' theorem:
$$P(H|E) = \frac{P(E|H) \cdot P(H)}{P(E)}$$
